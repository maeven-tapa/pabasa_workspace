About the platform

What is PABASA?

PABASA is a digital reading platform that helps learners practice aloud and gives educators clear guidance for supporting their progress.

Purpose

PABASA makes oral reading practice easier to guide, assess, and improve—helping every learner build fluency and confidence.

A simple learning loop

How It Works

  1. 01

    Teacher Assigns

    A teacher selects a reading activity for the learner.

  2. 02

    Student Reads

    The learner completes the activity by reading aloud.

  3. 03

    Progress Is Reviewed

    Results help identify strengths and areas for practice.

  4. 04

    Support Continues

    The next activity is guided by the learner’s needs.
