What is PABASA

Program for Automated Basic Reading and Speech Assessment

PABASA helps teachers and learners track fluency with automated reading checks, speech feedback, and clear next steps.

Why it matters

Turn assessment into a guided experience so students can practice with confidence while teachers get instant insights.

Automated checks

Capture reading accuracy and speed with structured passages.

Speech feedback

Provide pronunciation hints and supportive prompts.

Actionable reports

Summaries that highlight strengths and next practice targets.
