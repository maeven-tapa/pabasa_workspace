Please read these Terms of Use before accessing PABASA. By creating an account, joining a class, or otherwise using the platform, you agree to follow these terms. If you do not agree, do not use the platform.

Schools, teachers, parents, or guardians who authorize a learner’s use should review these terms with the learner in age-appropriate language.

01

Acceptance of these terms

These terms apply to PABASA’s public pages, accounts, classroom tools, reading activities, assessments, reports, and related services. Using any part of PABASA means that you accept these terms and any policies referenced on the platform.

02

Accounts and eligibility

  • Provide accurate information when registering and keep it reasonably current.
  • Use the account role that correctly describes you, such as student or teacher.
  • Keep passwords and one-time verification codes private.
  • Students should use PABASA only with authorization from their school, teacher, parent, or guardian, as appropriate.
  • You are responsible for activity performed through your account unless you promptly report unauthorized access.
03

Acceptable use

Use PABASA only for legitimate educational and classroom purposes. You must not:

  • Harass another person, impersonate someone, or provide deliberately false information.
  • Attempt to access another user’s account, class, records, or restricted administrative areas.
  • Upload malicious files, interfere with the service, bypass security controls, or test vulnerabilities without written authorization.
  • Use automated tools to scrape, overload, or reproduce substantial parts of the platform.
  • Submit unlawful, harmful, discriminatory, or inappropriate classroom content.
04

Content and reading materials

Teachers and authorized users may add or select reading materials for classroom activities. You remain responsible for ensuring that material you provide is accurate, age-appropriate, and permitted for your use. Do not upload content that violates another person’s copyright, privacy, or other rights.

You give PABASA permission to process submitted material only as needed to provide, secure, maintain, and improve the requested platform functions.

05

Information and privacy

PABASA processes account, class, activity, and reading-session information to operate its educational features. Users should provide only information that is necessary for those functions and should not place passwords, private family information, or unrelated sensitive data in free-text fields or uploaded materials.

Schools and educators are responsible for obtaining any permissions required for learner participation and for handling exported or copied records appropriately.

06

Educational use and professional judgment

PABASA is an educational support tool. Automated reading signals, summaries, and suggestions may contain errors and should not be treated as the sole basis for grades, placement, diagnosis, discipline, or other high-impact decisions. Teachers and authorized school personnel should review results in context and apply their professional judgment.

07

We aim to keep PABASA reliable, but we do not guarantee uninterrupted or error-free access. Features may be maintained, updated, limited, or temporarily unavailable. Keep appropriate backups of materials or records that your school needs outside the platform.
