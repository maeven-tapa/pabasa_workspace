Help

  • Open your course, then create a reading material manually, upload a file, or select a shared material from the Others tab.

  • Copy the class code from your dashboard and share it with your students so they can join your class. You can also add students manually from the Class page.

  • Practice materials are managed by the Admin account and cannot be created or edited by teachers.

  • Open your course, go to Reports & Updates, select the student(s), choose the update type, and send your report or update.

  • Open your course, create an assessment and click Live. All students in the course will automatically receive the assessment and begin together after the countdown.

  • Go to Profile → Account Security section → Toggle email and push notifications. You can also manage weekly digest preferences in the Preferences card.
