Help

  • Log in to your teacher dashboard, navigate to your Class, click Create Material, and upload your content (PDF, image, or text). Students will see it in their assignments.

  • Go to your Class → Share → Copy the class code. Share this code with students. They can join by entering the code in their Student Dashboard.

  • Select your class → Choose students → Create assignment → Select materials → Set due date → Send. Students will receive notifications about the new assignment.

  • From your Dashboard → Progress Reports → Select your class or specific student → View detailed reading analytics, comprehension metrics, and performance trends.

  • Go to Profile → Account Security section → Toggle email and push notifications. You can also manage weekly digest preferences in the Preferences card.
